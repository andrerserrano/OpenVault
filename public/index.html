How It Works

🔐
Policy-Gated Signing
Every trade passes through configurable risk policies before the OWS wallet signs. Max trade size, session budgets, token allowlists.
📋
AI Reasoning Audit Trail
Every trade attempt — approved or denied — is logged with the full AI research reasoning. Click any trade below to see why it was made.
⏱️
Session-Scoped Access
Time-limited OWS API keys per trading session. When the session ends, the key is revoked. Agents never touch raw private keys.
